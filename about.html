About

Hello there, my name is Aiden Riekenbrauck. I am a second year Interactive Media Design student at Fanshawe college. Aside from struggling in code driven courses, I seek to pursue a career in motion design and graphics. Currently I am working in the Cinema 4D environment using Octane Renderer to render out finalized products. As of recently I have aquired X-Particles 4.0 and plan to dive deep into learning the program.

Outside from school and motion design, I believe its very important to have a hobby or an activity that you enjoy. I enjoy going to the gym and working out, specifically training for powerlifting because of the dedication and commitment it requires.
